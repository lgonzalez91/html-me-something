What is makes a road bike?

Sheldon Brown gives a general definition of a road bike.

But if I were to define a road bike, it is a bike with "aggressive" geometry that is made with intention to ride on the road, or as Sheldon calls it, a sport bike.
Think the difference between a regular 4-door car and a sport car.

What makes a mountain bike?

If you follow this link, you'll notice I like referencing this guy.

The general "off-road" definition is a little generous, as you'll see with cyclocross, so I would define a modern mountain as a bike designed
with the intention of handling any type of terrain more extreme than your regular bike trail. Think the difference between walking around
Forest Park and walking up a mountain.

What makes a fixed gear/track bike?

Track bikes technically fall under fixed gear bikes.
However, Track racing and "Fixie" bikes encompass two very different disciplines.
